Apostle Islands Poster Series

Abstracted color and form are used in a grid to represent the marriage between lake, sandstone, and foliage across the Archipelago in a 12 piece poster series.

Concept 01

The Apostle Islands embody a balance between water and land through 3 stark elementsâ€”a cerulean blue lake, jutting red-orange sandstone, and lush green foliage. The marriage of these features across the Archipelago is displayed within a split complementary range of color.

Concept 02

Numerous lighthouses sit along the shores of the Apostle Islands. Every night, they reach into the sky and illuminate the lakeshore. This is an exploration of how this display of color interacts with the bay-line at night utilizing a double complementary color palette.
